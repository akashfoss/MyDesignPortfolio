VISITING CARD, FLOW

This is my first design project with a community called Flow, a knowledge sharing community of self directed learners.

I was in search of some projects based on UI and here I got a contact with the CEO of Flow, Mr. Harshit Jaiswal. Under his guidance I worked on this project.

At first Harshit gave a brief description about his company and also some details regarding how this community works. Then we had a call to discuss about the design.

During this time I was also doing a course on UI specialization from coursera, and with that knowledge I started designing this project and drew whatever came in my mind.

Then I tried to implement those things. Before I proceed further I would like to say the most difficult part *Design should be minimal and well structured*. After hearing this I had to change everything I designed and moved to canva.

I knew somewhat about canva but I was not having much experience with it. I started using canva and got 1000+ ideas to design but then going back to the user conditions, I had to make my own design and I started designing on vistaprint.

After a lot of searches about designing, I moved to vistaprint (but never used their ideas). I created my own design within 1 hour and I was like oh half of the work is done (i.e only the front part), I was not having any plan to design the back part as I thought only front looking cards are always good. But I was wrong, I sent this design to Harshit and he was very happy to see that. But still something was missing there, he said. Then I thought to design the back part of the card and started with the community quotes.

But then an idea came in mind of adding QR code linked with the community site and I implemented it too. Now this design was looking super cool as per Harshit. And we decided to move further with the same design.
